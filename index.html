What is The Fan Engagement

The Fan Engagement is an SMS-powered frictionless user engagement platform that brings brands right to the fingertips of fans during live sports events.

Linked with real-time stats and data, our gamified platform allows brands to engage sports fans in fun activities during a live match. They can try to predict in-game action and outcomes, or challenge themselves with trivia.
